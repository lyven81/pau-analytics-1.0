What to Expect

What Happens When You Work With Us

A plain-language analyst report, an interactive dashboard you can filter and explore, and — on higher tiers — an AI assistant that answers your business questions on demand.

Your business data (usually a spreadsheet or CSV export), a clear question you're trying to answer, and about 30 minutes for a kickoff call to align on scope.

You receive the report, dashboard link, and any AI assistant credentials. Everything is yours to keep. We walk you through how to use it and answer follow-up questions.

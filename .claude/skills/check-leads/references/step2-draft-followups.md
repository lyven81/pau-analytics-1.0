# Step 2 — Draft WhatsApp Follow-Up Messages

---

## 2A: Read Brand Voice

Read `C:\Users\Lenovo\Documents\Pau Analytics\Foundation - Who we are\brand-voice-guidelines.md` before drafting any messages.

Key rules for WhatsApp messages:
- Speak like a smart, trustworthy friend — not a consultant
- Short sentences, everyday words
- Use "you/your", active voice
- No jargon, no corporate filler
- One clear next step per message

---

## 2B: Draft Messages

For each lead identified in Step 1, draft a WhatsApp follow-up message.

### Message structure by channel:

**CH-A leads (Google Ads — phone number available):**
```
Hi [name], this is Yih Ven from Pau Analytics.

You mentioned [paraphrase their challenge in 1 sentence].

I've worked on similar problems with [F&B/retail/e-commerce] businesses — [one specific example or outcome in 1 sentence].

Would a quick 15-minute call help? I can share what I've seen work.

No pressure — just reply here if you're interested.
```

**CH-B leads (Blog — no phone number):**
```
Hi [name], thanks for reaching out through the blog.

You mentioned [paraphrase their challenge in 1 sentence].

That's exactly the kind of problem we help [business type] owners solve — [one concrete thing Pau Analytics does that addresses the challenge].

If you'd like to explore this, here's a link to book a free 20-minute call:
https://tidycal.com/pauanalytics/discovery

No commitment — just a conversation.
```

**CH-W leads (Chat widget — may or may not have phone):**
```
Hi [name], this is Yih Ven. You chatted with Jason on our website earlier.

I saw you asked about [paraphrase their challenge].

I'd love to help. Would a short call work? You can pick a time here:
https://tidycal.com/pauanalytics/discovery

Or just reply here and we can continue the conversation.
```

### Personalization rules:
- Always use the lead's actual name
- Always reference their specific challenge (not a generic pitch)
- If the lead came from a specific blog post (CH-B), mention the topic: "I saw you were reading about [blog topic]"
- If urgency score is 4-5, add: "I noticed this seems time-sensitive — happy to jump on a call today if that helps."
- Keep messages under 100 words

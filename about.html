The Founder

Lee Yih Ven — Data Analyst & Consultant

I help Malaysian SME owners make sense of their business data. I work with owners and managers in retail, F&B, property, and services who have data sitting in spreadsheets — but no clear picture of what it means or what to do next.

Every project I take on starts with a business question, not a dataset. That distinction matters. The analysis only has value if it connects to a decision you need to make.

I cover six types of analysis: predictive modelling, regression, statistical testing, segmentation, optimization, and time series — each tied to the kind of questions Malaysian SME owners actually ask.

Pau Analytics is a sister company to Pau AI, which builds custom AI systems for Malaysian businesses. Together, they cover the full picture — from analysing what's happened to automating what happens next.

Our Principles

Three Things We Never Compromise On

🎯

Data Over Guesswork

Every recommendation is grounded in what the numbers actually show. If the data contradicts the assumption, the assumption loses — every time.

💬

Clarity Over Complexity

The goal is a decision, not a dissertation. Reports are written in plain language. Charts show one thing clearly. Jargon stays out.

🔑

You Own Your Insights

Every report, dashboard, and AI assistant we build is yours. Your data stays with you. We don't retain rights to anything we deliver.
